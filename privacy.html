Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Luzerge ("we", "us", or "our") operates luzerge.com. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive from Google:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your Google profile picture

When you use the Cache Manager dashboard, we store:

  • Domain names you add to your account
  • Cloudflare Zone IDs and API tokens you provide
  • A log of cache purge actions you perform

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To identify your account and display your domains
  • To perform Cloudflare cache purge operations on your behalf
  • To show you your purge history

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties except as required to operate the service (Supabase for database hosting).

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Supabase (Singapore region). Access is protected by Row Level Security — you can only access your own data. Cloudflare API tokens are stored encrypted at rest.

4. Google OAuth

We use Google Sign-In solely for authentication. We request only your basic profile and email. We do not access your Google Drive, Gmail, or any other Google services. You can revoke access at any time via Google Account Permissions .

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use Supabase's authentication library which stores your session token in browser local storage. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by emailing hello@luzerge.com. We will process deletion requests within 7 business days.

7. Contact

For any privacy questions, contact us at hello@luzerge.com
