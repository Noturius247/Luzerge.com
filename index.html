How It Works

From domain to live site in 3–5 business days.

  1. 01

    Buy Your Domain

    We register your .com domain through Cloudflare Registrar at cost (~$8.57/yr). You own it fully.

  2. 02

    Connect Cloudflare CDN

    We configure DNS, SSL, caching rules, and WAF protection. Cebu users hit the Singapore edge node — under 60ms.

  3. 03

    Deploy Your Site

    We build and push your site to GitHub. Every future update auto-deploys and purges the CDN cache.

  4. 04

    Go Live & Grow

    You get a monthly performance report showing cache hit rates, speed scores, and leads captured.
