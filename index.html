Common Questions

From Cebu business owners like you.

Kailangan ba nako mag-alam sa tech para mag-avail?

Dili! Kami bahala sa tanan. Mag-send lang ug imong logo, photos, ug imong gusto isulti sa imong website — kami na ang bahala sa uban.

Do I need technical knowledge to get a website?

No. We handle everything from design to launch. Just send us your business info, photos, and logo — we do the rest.

Why is there no setup fee?

We removed setup fees because we know it's a big barrier for small businesses. Instead, we earn through the monthly subscription — so we're motivated to keep you happy long-term.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! Your first month is completely free — no payment needed upfront. After that, cancel anytime. No lock-in contracts. If you're not happy, just let us know and we'll stop billing you. You keep your domain.

What if I already have a website that's slow?

We can migrate your existing website and speed it up — often cutting load time by 50–80%. Contact us for a free speed audit.

How do I pay? GCash ba?

Yes! We accept GCash, Maya, bank transfer (BDO/BPI/UnionBank), and cash. Monthly billing — pay when you're satisfied each month.

How long does it take to build my website?

3–5 business days for Starter and Business plans. Online stores take 7–10 days. We'll keep you updated throughout.

Do you serve clients outside Cebu?

Yes! We work with businesses all over the Philippines. We communicate via Viber, email, and Zoom — no need to meet in person.
